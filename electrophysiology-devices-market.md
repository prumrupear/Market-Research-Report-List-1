<p><ul><li>Monitoring Devices</li><li>Treatment Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrophysiology devices market comprises two primary types: monitoring devices and treatment devices. Monitoring devices are designed to assess electrical activity in the heart, aiding in the diagnosis of arrhythmias and other cardiac conditions. They often include electrocardiograms (ECGs) and implantable loop recorders. Treatment devices focus on delivering therapies to manage or correct arrhythmias, including ablation catheters and implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs). Together, these devices enhance patient management and outcomes in electrophysiology.</p></p>

<p><ul><li> Single Lumen Implantable Port</li><li>Double Lumen Implantable Port </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Implantable Venous Access System market primarily features two types: single lumen and double lumen implantable ports. Single lumen ports consist of one access channel, ideal for patients requiring limited intravenous therapy or medication infusions. In contrast, double lumen ports have two separate channels, allowing simultaneous administration of multiple therapies or for drawing blood and providing medication, enhancing versatility in treatment. Both types improve patient comfort, reduce the need for repeated venipunctures, and are commonly used in oncology and chronic disease management.</p></p>

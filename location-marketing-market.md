<p><ul><li>Indoor Geo-Marketing</li><li>Outdoor Geo-Marketing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Location marketing encompasses strategies that leverage geographic data to reach consumers effectively. Indoor geo-marketing targets customers within buildings, utilizing technologies like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and beacons to deliver personalized messages and promotions based on their precise location. In contrast, outdoor geo-marketing focuses on engaging consumers in public spaces through location-based advertising on mobile devices and digital billboards, often utilizing GPS technology. Both types aim to enhance customer engagement by providing relevant content at the right time and place.</p></p>

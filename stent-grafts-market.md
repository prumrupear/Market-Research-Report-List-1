<p><p>The stent grafts market is primarily segmented into AAA (Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm) and TAA (Thoracic Aortic Aneurysm) stent grafts. AAA stent grafts are designed to treat aneurysms in the abdominal aorta, providing support and reinforcing the vessel wall to prevent rupture. TAA stent grafts target aneurysms in the thoracic aorta, similar in function but tailored for the upper aorta. Both types are critical for minimizing surgical risks and ensuring effective treatment for patients with vascular diseases.</p></p>

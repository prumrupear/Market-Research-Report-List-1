<p><p>The fiber supplements powder market includes various types such as inulin, methylcellulose, psyllium, and wheat dextrin. Inulin is derived from plants and aids digestion and gut health. Methylcellulose is a synthetic soluble fiber that promotes regularity without fermenting in the gut. Psyllium, sourced from psyllium husks, is known for its ability to relieve constipation and support heart health. Wheat dextrin is a soluble fiber that enhances digestive health and is often used to improve taste and texture in food products.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Single Lumen</li><li>Double Lumen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oocyte retrieval needles market is segmented into single lumen and double lumen types. Single lumen needles feature a single channel for aspiration, making them straightforward and efficient for collecting oocytes during in vitro fertilization (IVF) procedures. Conversely, double lumen needles have two channels, allowing for simultaneous aspiration and flushing, which can enhance the retrieval process by improving oocyte yield and reducing damage. Each type caters to specific clinical needs, influencing their adoption based on procedural preferences.</p></p>

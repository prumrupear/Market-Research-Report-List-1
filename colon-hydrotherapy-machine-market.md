<p><p>The colon hydrotherapy machine market is divided into two main types: open systems and closed systems. Open systems allow water to flow into the colon through a nozzle, enabling self-regulation of the process, while offering a more comfortable experience. Closed systems use a professional unit that provides controlled water infusion and waste removal through a sealed mechanism, ensuring efficiency and sanitation. Both systems aim to promote colon health and detoxification but differ in user experience and operational methods.</p></p>

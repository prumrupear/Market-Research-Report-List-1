<p><p>The medical ultrasound gel market comprises two main types: sterile and non-sterile gels. Sterile ultrasound gel is specifically designed for use in invasive procedures, ensuring a high level of cleanliness to prevent infections. It is typically packaged in single-use containers to maintain sterility. Non-sterile ultrasound gel, on the other hand, is used for routine imaging procedures and can be packaged in larger bottles for multiple uses. Both types facilitate effective sound wave transmission, essential for accurate imaging in diagnostics.</p></p>

<p><p>The Primary Hip Replacement System market includes various implant types based on the bearing surface materials. Metal-on-Polyethylene (MoP) combines metals with a polyethylene liner, offering durability. Metal-on-Metal (MoM) features metal-on-metal components for improved wear resistance but may pose risks. Ceramic-on-Polyethylene (CoP) uses a ceramic ball with a polyethylene socket, known for low wear. Ceramic-on-Ceramic (CoC) provides excellent wear properties and less debris, while Ceramic-on-Metal (CoM) combines the benefits of ceramics with metal surfaces for enhanced performance.</p></p>

Cholinesterase inhibitors are therapeutic agents that enhance cognitive functions by preventing the breakdown of acetylcholine. NMDA receptor antagonists address neuronal excitotoxicity, providing neuroprotection. Brain imaging techniques, such as PET and MRI, visualize brain changes associated with Alzheimer’s. Additionally, emerging blood tests offer a non-invasive approach to detect biomarkers, streamlining early diagnosis and treatment strategies.</p></p>

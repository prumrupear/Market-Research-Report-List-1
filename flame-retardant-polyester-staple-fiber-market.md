<p><p>Flame Retardant Polyester Staple Fiber (FRP) is categorized into two main types: Inherent FRP Staple Fiber and Treated FRP Staple Fiber. Inherent FRP Staple Fiber possesses flame-retardant properties intrinsically due to its chemical structure, providing enhanced safety without additional treatments. In contrast, Treated FRP Staple Fiber undergoes a chemical coating or application process to achieve flame resistance. Both types are crucial in industries requiring fire safety, such as textiles, automotive, and construction, responding to stringent safety regulations.</p></p>

<p><p>The commercial ovens market is primarily divided into gas and electric ovens. Gas ovens utilize natural gas or propane, offering quicker temperature adjustments and often lower operating costs, making them popular in high-volume kitchens. Electric ovens, on the other hand, provide consistent heat distribution and are easier to maintain, making them suitable for establishments requiring precise cooking. Both types cater to various culinary needs, influencing their adoption based on operational preferences, energy availability, and menu requirements.</p></p>

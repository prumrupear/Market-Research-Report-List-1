<p><p>The tow tractors market comprises three main types: pedestrian towing tractors, stand-in towing tractors, and rider-seated towing tractors. Pedestrian towing tractors are designed for operators who walk alongside the machine, providing maneuverability in tight spaces. Stand-in towing tractors allow operators to stand while driving, offering a balance of mobility and comfort for short-distance tasks. Rider-seated towing tractors feature a seated operator position, ideal for longer distances and heavier loads, enhancing efficiency in material handling and logistics operations.</p></p>

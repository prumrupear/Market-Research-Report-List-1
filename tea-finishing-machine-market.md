<p><p>The tea finishing machine market encompasses various types, each catering to different processing needs. Roller finishing machines use mechanical rollers to twist and shape tea leaves, enhancing flavor. Microwave finishing machines utilize microwave energy for quick drying and preserving essential oils. Steam finishing machines apply steam to soften leaves and reduce astringency, while hot wind finishing machines utilize hot air to achieve a consistent drying effect. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing tea quality and production efficiency.</p></p>

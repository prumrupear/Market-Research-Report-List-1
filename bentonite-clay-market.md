<p><p>The Bentonite clay market is categorized into three main types: Sodium Bentonite, Calcium Bentonite, and Potassium Bentonite. Sodium Bentonite is known for its high absorption properties, making it ideal for drilling and sealing applications. Calcium Bentonite is valued for its adsorbent qualities, often used in cosmetics and pharmaceutical products. Potassium Bentonite, with its exceptional swelling capabilities, is primarily used in agriculture as a soil amendment. Each type serves distinct industrial applications, driving diverse market demand.</p></p>

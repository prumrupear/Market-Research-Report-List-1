<p><p>Plant water monitors are essential tools for assessing soil moisture levels, crucial for healthy plant growth. The market segments into two primary types: single parameter instruments and multiparameter instruments. Single parameter instruments focus on measuring one specific aspect, such as soil moisture, offering simplicity and ease of use.

<p><p>The residential electric water heaters market is primarily divided into two types: tank electric water heaters and tankless electric water heaters. Tank electric water heaters store and heat a fixed volume of water, providing a readily available supply but facing limitations in continuous hot water availability. In contrast, tankless electric water heaters heat water on demand, offering endless hot water while being more energy-efficient, as they only operate when needed. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences and needs for hot water supply.</p></p>

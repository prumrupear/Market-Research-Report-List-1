<p><strong>The Peptide Synthesizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid-Phase Peptide Synthesis</li><li>Solution-Phase Peptide Synthesis</li><li>Hybrid & Recombinant Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The peptide synthesizers market is categorized into three main types. Solid-Phase Peptide Synthesis (SPPS) involves attaching the peptide chain to a solid resin, facilitating easier purification. Solution-Phase Peptide Synthesis occurs in a liquid medium, allowing for larger peptides but often requiring extensive purification. Hybrid technology combines elements of both solid and solution phases, optimizing efficiency and yield. Recombinant technology leverages living cells to produce peptides, offering a biological route for peptide synthesis with potential for complex modifications.</p></p>

<p><p>The automotive transmission filter market is divided into two main types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and aftermarket segments. OEM filters are produced by vehicle manufacturers or their authorized suppliers, ensuring compatibility and quality for new vehicles. Aftermarket filters, on the other hand, are independently manufactured and available for purchase to replace worn or damaged filters in existing vehicles, often providing cost-effective alternatives. Both segments cater to different consumer needs, focusing on performance and maintenance within the automotive industry.</p></p>

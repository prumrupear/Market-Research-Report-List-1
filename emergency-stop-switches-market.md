<p><p>The Emergency Stop Switches market encompasses three key types: mounting bracket, pendant, and push-turn switches. Mounting bracket switches are designed for easy installation on machinery or panels, providing quick access. Pendant switches are typically suspended from cables, allowing operators to activate the emergency stop from a distance, ideal for larger equipment. Push-turn switches combine the function of a push button with a rotational mechanism, ensuring reliability in emergency situations. These switches are crucial for enhancing safety in industrial environments.</p></p>

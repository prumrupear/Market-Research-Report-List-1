<p><p>Soluble corn fiber is a type of dietary fiber derived from corn that is soluble in water. It is used as a bulking agent, sweetener, and thickener in various food and beverage applications. The ingredient is prized for its prebiotic properties, which support digestive health, and its ability to add texture without significantly impacting calorie content. Soluble corn fiber is increasingly incorporated into products such as baked goods, dairy items, snacks, and beverages to enhance fiber content and improve overall nutritional profiles.</p><p>The soluble corn fiber market is witnessing significant growth, driven by rising consumer health awareness and demand for fiber-enriched products.

<p><p>The radioimmunoassay (RIA) kits market comprises three main types: pure antigen, radio-labeled antigen, and antibody. Pure antigen kits utilize untagged antigens to detect specific antibodies in a sample, ensuring high specificity. Radio-labeled antigen kits incorporate radioactive isotopes into antigens, enhancing sensitivity and enabling quantitative measurements. Antibody kits focus on the use of specific antibodies to bind target antigens, offering robust detection capabilities. Each type plays a crucial role in various applications, including diagnostics and research.</p></p>

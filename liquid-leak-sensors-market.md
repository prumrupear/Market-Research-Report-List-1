<p><ul><li>Invasive Leak Detection</li><li>Non-Invasive Leak Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid leak sensor market is divided into two main types: invasive and non-invasive leak detection. Invasive leak detection involves the installation of sensors directly within the environment that monitors for leaks, often offering high sensitivity and accuracy. In contrast, non-invasive leak detection monitors external conditions and fluid changes without physical contact with the liquid. This method provides a quick and less disruptive way to identify leaks, making it suitable for a variety of applications, including residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>

<p><strong>The BLDC Electric Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Motor</li><li>Outer Rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The BLDC (Brushless Direct Current) electric motor market is primarily categorized into inner rotor and outer rotor types. Inner rotor motors have the rotor inside the stator, offering compact design and high RPM, making them suitable for applications like drones and robotics. Outer rotor motors have the rotor surrounding the stator, providing higher torque at lower speeds, which is ideal for applications such as electric bikes and fans. Both types are favored for their efficiency and reduced maintenance requirements.</p></p>

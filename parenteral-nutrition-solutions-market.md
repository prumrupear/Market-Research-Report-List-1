<p><strong>The Parenteral Nutrition Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Large Volume Parenteral (LVP)</li><li>Small Volume Parenteral (SVP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parenteral nutrition solutions are categorized into two main types: Large Volume Parenteral (LVP) and Small Volume Parenteral (SVP). LVP refers to intravenous solutions typically exceeding 100 mL, used for fluid replacement and nutrient supply over an extended period, often in clinical settings. In contrast, SVP involves smaller volumes, typically under 100 mL, designed for delivering medications, vitamins, or supplements more concentratedly, often in outpatient and home care scenarios. Both play vital roles in patient care and nutritional management.</p></p>

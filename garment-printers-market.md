<p><strong>The Garment Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Screen Printers</li><li>Rotary Screen Printers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The garment printers market is primarily divided into flat screen printers and rotary screen printers. Flat screen printers utilize a stationary screen for printing designs directly onto fabric, offering versatility and precision for small to medium runs. In contrast, rotary screen printers employ cylindrical screens that continuously feed fabric through, making them ideal for high-volume production with consistent patterns. Both types cater to different production needs, with flat screen printers favored for custom work and rotary screens for mass manufacturing.</p></p>

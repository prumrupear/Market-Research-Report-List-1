<p><strong>The Airport GPU Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mobile GPU</li><li>Fixed GPU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airport GPU (Ground Power Unit) market consists of two main types: mobile and fixed GPUs. Mobile GPUs are versatile, easily transportable units used in various locations on the airport ramp to provide power and support to aircraft during maintenance or while parked. In contrast, fixed GPUs are permanently installed systems that deliver consistent power supply to aircraft at specific gates, enhancing operational efficiency and reducing the need for onboard auxiliary power units. Both types play crucial roles in airport ground support operations.</p></p>

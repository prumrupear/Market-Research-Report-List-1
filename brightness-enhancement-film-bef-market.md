<p><p>Brightness Enhancement Film (BEF) is designed to improve the brightness of LCD displays by redirecting light. The market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Normal Prism** enhances brightness by controlling light direction through prismatic structures. </p><p>2. **Multi-function Prism** not only boosts brightness but also offers features like glare reduction. </p><p>3. **Micro Lens Film** utilizes micro-lens technology to focus light more effectively. </p><p>4. **DBEF (Dual Brightness Enhancement Film)** employs dual layers for superior brightness control and efficiency, catering to various display applications.</p></p>

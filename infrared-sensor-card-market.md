<p><p>The infrared sensor card market encompasses various types of sensors based on their operational wavelengths: Infrared (IR), Near Infrared (NIR), Ultraviolet (UV), and Visible (VIS) sensors. IR sensors detect thermal radiation and are often used in night vision and temperature measurement. NIR sensors operate in the short-wave infrared spectrum, commonly employed in agricultural monitoring and food quality analysis. UV sensors measure ultraviolet light for applications such as water disinfection and environmental monitoring, while VIS sensors capture light in the visible spectrum for imaging and display technologies. Each type serves distinct industries and applications, enhancing functionality across various fields.</p></p>

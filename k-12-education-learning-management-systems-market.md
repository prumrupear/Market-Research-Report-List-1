<p><strong>The K-12 Education Learning Management Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-Based</li><li>On-Premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>K-12 Education Learning Management Systems (LMS) can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based LMS solutions are hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, ease of use, and automatic updates. On-premise LMS, however, are installed locally on a school's own servers, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance and updates. Both types aim to enhance educational delivery and administration in K-12 institutions.</p></p>

<p><p>Visitor Management Systems (VMS) are categorized into two primary types: on-premise VMS and cloud-based VMS. On-premise VMS is installed locally on an organization's servers, offering greater control and customization, but may require significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based VMS operates on remote servers, providing accessibility from anywhere, scalability, and lower initial costs, as it typically follows a subscription model. Organizations choose between these types based on their specific needs for security, flexibility, and budget.</p></p>

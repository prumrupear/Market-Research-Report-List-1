<p><p>The industrial plastic 3D printer market includes various technologies, primarily Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes a laser to fuse powdered plastic layer by layer, producing dense and complex parts suitable for demanding applications. EBM employs electron beams in a vacuum to melt plastic powder, allowing for high build rates and better thermal management. Other market types encompass diverse additive manufacturing techniques, each offering unique benefits for specific industrial needs, such as robustness, speed, or precision.</p></p>

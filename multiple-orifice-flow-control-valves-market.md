<p><p>Multiple Orifice Flow Control Valves are classified into two main market types: manual and automatic. Manual flow control valves require operator intervention to adjust flow rates, providing precise control in applications where adjustments are infrequent. In contrast, automatic flow control valves utilize sensors and actuators to regulate flow without manual input, enhancing efficiency and responsiveness in dynamic systems. Each type serves distinct needs, with manual valves often favored for simplicity and lower costs, while automatic valves offer advanced control for complex operations.</p></p>

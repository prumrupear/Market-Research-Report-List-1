<p><strong>The Automatic Bilge Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Switch</li><li>No Float Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic bilge pump market is categorized primarily into two types: float switch and no float switch systems. Float switch pumps operate using a buoyant device that activates the pump when water reaches a certain level, ensuring efficient water removal from the bilge. In contrast, no float switch pumps employ electronic sensors or continuous monitoring systems to detect water levels and activate the pump, offering a more advanced and potentially more reliable solution. Together, these technologies enhance vessel safety and efficiency.</p></p>

<p><p>A hard busbar is a metallic conductor used in electrical distribution systems to connect various components within a power system. Typically made of copper or aluminum, hard busbars offer low resistance and high thermal conductivity, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications in substations, industrial installations, and data centers. Their reliability and efficiency in transferring large amounts of electrical current have driven their adoption across various sectors.</p><p>The Hard Busbar Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.1% during the forecast period, fueled by increasing demand for efficient power distribution and management systems.

<p><p>Harmonic gearboxes are categorized into three main styles: cup style, hat style, and pancake style. Cup style gearboxes feature a compact, circular design, ideal for applications requiring high torque in limited spaces. Hat style gearboxes possess a unique, extended structure that enhances load distribution and efficiency, suitable for robotics and automation. Pancake style gearboxes are characterized by their low profile and lightweight design, making them perfect for applications where height constraints are critical, such as in aerospace and advanced machinery.</p></p>
